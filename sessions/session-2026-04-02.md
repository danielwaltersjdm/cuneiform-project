- **Primary corpus going forward: Kanesh (Old Assyrian merchant texts)**, not Neo-Assyrian SAA letters. SAA letters are royal correspondence with numbers mostly representing troop counts — poor fit for behavioral analysis.
- **Translation gap is the binding constraint**: only 67/4,775 Kanesh tablets have English translations in the downloaded ATF file. LLM-based translation of Akkadian transliterations identified as the highest-leverage next step.
- **Research priority order**: (1) conditional clause framing → prospect theory; (2) sentiment asymmetry → loss aversion; (3) price expectation vs. outcome matching → overconfidence
- SAA oracle/astrological volumes (SAA04, SAA08, SAA09, SAA10) noted as a secondary corpus worth downloading for overconfidence/forecast research
